The Rudest Book Ever : Shwetabh Gangwar




Rating : 5 / 5


Summary :-

This book is a collection of perspectives and ideas on freeing your mind from all bullshit. The truth of life you would rarely hear from parents, friends, society, or the internet. Shwetabh has opinions as everyone in the world tends to have. Every piece of information available now is just the product of someone’s ideas and thoughts. You can have your own opinions and ideas about everything you can come up with.

Shwetabh Gangwar is here to solve your problems. In his book, The Rudest Book Ever, he cuts through the clutter and offers simple, practical solutions which will help us lead better lives.

He believes that all people need is a set of principles to guide them through the process and the journey of life will be infinitely more convenient. This book is published by Westland.


Review :-

A book that perfectly resonated with and reflected me after a long time.

There are also some of the things that I didn’t like about the book. You do not have to always use swear words to make a powerful impact. Sometimes that sounds very artificial. People might wish for little more depth in some of the issues in which brevity wasn’t desired. There might be many other issues and promises where Shwetabh has failed to deliver but certainly not on this one.
Read. Work. Shine…

Inspite all of that, I'll give this book a 5/5 rating for its out of the box, mind blowing, and practical life hacks & perspectives. They're truly worth more than these few of the flaws, according to me.
