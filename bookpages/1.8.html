The 5 AM Club : Robin Sharma




Rating : 4.5 / 5


Summary :-

Legendary leadership and elite performance expert Robin Sharma introduced The 5am Club concept over twenty years ago, based on a revolutionary morning routine that has helped his clients maximize their productivity, activate their best health and bulletproof their serenity in this age of overwhelming complexity.

The 5 AM Club helps you get up at 5 AM every morning, build a morning routine, and make time for the self-improvement you need to find success. You wake up to your alarm, press snooze, and roll back over for an extra few minutes of sleep.

Now, in this life-changing book, handcrafted by the author over a rigorous four-year period, you will discover the early-rising habit that has helped so many accomplish epic results while upgrading their happiness, helpfulness and feelings of aliveness. Through an enchanting--and often amusing--story about two struggling strangers who meet an eccentric tycoon who becomes their secret mentor

The 5am Club will walk you through: A neuroscience-based practice proven to help make it easy to rise while most people are sleeping, giving you precious time for yourself to think, express your creativity and begin the day peacefully, leading your productivity levels to its peak.


Review :-

I would like to describe it as one of the mind changing book. Like it has really changed my perspective towards everything like towards world, towards people and most importantly towards my work.

The way all the learning models are explained beautifully yet in a simpler manner backed by scientific research is one of the key point of the book.

I just really enjoyed reading it and noting many great insights. One of my favorite is "it is in quietude that self awareness rises. It's really an wonderful and worthy Book; Which really deserves a rating of 4.5/5.
